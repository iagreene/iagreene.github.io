It is important to stay healthy, not just physically, but mentally!

Many high schoolers suffer from depression. This can be as a result of many things, including social and academic pressures

Teenagers are often left to suffer on their own, and most do not receive the treatment and support they need, such as therapists, guidance counselors, and even friends
